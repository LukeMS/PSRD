Pain Strike¶

Schoolevocation [evil]; Levelsorcerer/wizard 3, witch 3

Casting Time1 standard action

ComponentsV, S

Rangeclose (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

Targetone living creature

Duration1 round/level (D)

Saving ThrowFortitude negates; Spell Resistanceyes

Pain strike racks the targeted creature with agony, inflicting 1d6 points of nonlethal damage per round for 1 round per level (maximum 10 rounds). Additionally, the affected creature is sickened for the spell’s duration, and the caster gains a +4 circumstance bonus on Intimidate checks against the target.

Pain Strike, Mass¶

Schoolevocation [evil]; Levelsorcerer/wizard 5, witch 5

Targetsone living creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

This spell works like pain strike, except as noted above.
