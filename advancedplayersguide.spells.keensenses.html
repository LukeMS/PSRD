Keen Senses¶

Schooltransmutation; Levelalchemist 1, druid 1, ranger 1

Casting Time1 standard action

ComponentsV, M/DF (a hawk’s feather)

Rangetouch

Targetcreature touched

Duration1 minute/level (D)

Saving ThrowWill negates (harmless); Spell Resistanceyes (harmless)

The subject gains a +2 competence bonus on Perception checks and gains low-light vision. Subjects that have low-light vision double the distance they can see under the effects of this spell.
