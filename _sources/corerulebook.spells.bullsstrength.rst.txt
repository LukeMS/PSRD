Bull's Strength
================

\ **School**\  transmutation; \ **Level**\  cleric 2, druid 2, paladin 2, sorcerer/wizard 2

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, M/DF (a few hairs, or a pinch of dung, from a bull)

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  creature touched

\ **Duration**\  1 min./level

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates (harmless); :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes (harmless)

The subject becomes stronger. The spell grants a +4 enhancement bonus to :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , adding the usual benefits to melee attack rolls, melee damage rolls, and other uses of the :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\  modifier.

Bull's Strength, Mass
======================

\ **School**\  transmutation; \ **Level**\  cleric 6, druid 6, sorcerer/wizard 6

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Targets**\  one creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

This spell functions like :ref:`bull's strength <corerulebook.spells.bullsstrength#bull_s_strength>`\ , except that it affects multiple creatures.
