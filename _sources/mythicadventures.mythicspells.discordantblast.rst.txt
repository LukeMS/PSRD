Discordant Blast
=================

\ **Source**\  :ref:`discordant blast <advancedplayersguide.spells.discordantblast#discordant_blast>`

The damage dealt increases to 5d6 points of sonic damage, and you gain a +2 bonus on combat maneuver checks to the bull rush opponents. The spell can penetrate non-mythic magical silence and dispels any non-mythic :ref:`silence <corerulebook.spells.silence#silence>`\  effect of 4th level or lower.
