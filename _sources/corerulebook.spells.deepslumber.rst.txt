Deep Slumber
=============

\ **School**\  enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  bard 3, sorcerer/wizard 3

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

This spell functions like :ref:`sleep <corerulebook.spells.sleep#sleep>`\ , except that it affects 10 HD of targets.
