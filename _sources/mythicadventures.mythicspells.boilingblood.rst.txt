Boiling Blood
==============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`boiling blood <ultimatemagic.spells.boilingblood#boiling_blood>`

A target that fails its save takes 1d3 points of fire damage per round. If a target has the :ref:`orc <bestiary.creaturetypes#orc_subtype>`\  subtype, it also gains fire resistance 5.

\ **Augmented**\ : If you expend two uses of mythic power, you can affect allies as though they had the orc subtype.
