Black Mark
===========

\ **Source**\  :ref:`black mark <advancedraceguide.coreraces.humans#black_mark>`

Any time the cursed creature travels on or in the water more than 1 mile from shore, each day it (or the vessel it's on) is attacked by one or more :ref:`aquatic <bestiary.creaturetypes#aquatic_subtype>`\  or water monsters, as if summoned by :ref:`summon nature's ally VII <corerulebook.spells.summonnaturesally#summon_nature_s_ally_vii>`\  (caster level 13th).
