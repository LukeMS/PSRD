Magic Circle against Chaos
===========================

\ **School**\  abjuration [lawful]; \ **Level**\  cleric 3, paladin 3, sorcerer/wizard 3

This spell functions like :ref:`magic circle against evil <corerulebook.spells.magiccircleagainstevil#magic_circle_against_evil>`\ , except that it is similar to :ref:`protection from chaos <corerulebook.spells.protectionfromchaos#protection_from_chaos>`\  instead of :ref:`protection from evil <corerulebook.spells.protectionfromevil#protection_from_evil>`\ , and it can imprison a nonlawful called creature.
