Protection from Law, Communal
==============================

\ **Level**\  antipaladin 2, cleric 2, inquisitor 2, sorcerer/wizard 2, summoner 2

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`protection from law <corerulebook.spells.protectionfromlaw#protection_from_law>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
