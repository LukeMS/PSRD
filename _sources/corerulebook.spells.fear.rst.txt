Fear
=====

\ **School**\  necromancy [fear, mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  bard 3, sorcerer/wizard 4

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, M (the heart of a hen or a white feather)

\ **Range**\  30 ft.

\ **Area**\  cone-shaped burst

\ **Duration**\  1 round/level or 1 round; see text

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  partial; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

An invisible cone of terror causes each living creature in the area to become :ref:`panicked <corerulebook.glossary#panicked>`\  unless it succeeds on a :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  save. If cornered, a :ref:`panicked <corerulebook.glossary#panicked>`\  creature begins cowering. If the :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  save succeeds, the creature is :ref:`shaken <corerulebook.glossary#shaken>`\  for 1 round.
