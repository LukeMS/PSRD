Air Walk, Communal
===================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 5, cleric 5, druid 5

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`air walk <corerulebook.spells.airwalk#air_walk>`\ , except divide the duration in 10-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
