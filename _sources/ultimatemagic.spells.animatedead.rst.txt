Animate Dead, Lesser
=====================

\ **School**\  necromancy [evil]; \ **Level**\  cleric 2, sorcerer/wizard 3

\ **Target**\  one corpse

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

This spell functions as :ref:`animate dead <corerulebook.spells.animatedead#animate_dead>`\ , except you can only create a single Small or Medium skeleton or zombie. You cannot create variant skeletons or zombies with this spell.
