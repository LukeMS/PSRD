Spell Resistance
=================

\ **School**\  abjuration; \ **Level**\  cleric 5

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, DF

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  creature touched

\ **Duration**\  1 min./level

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates (harmless); :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes (harmless)

The target gains :ref:`spell resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  equal to 12 + your caster level.
