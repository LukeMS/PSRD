Ant Haul, Communal
===================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 2, cleric 2, druid 2, ranger 2, sorcerer/wizard 2, summoner 2

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`ant haul <advancedplayersguide.spells.anthaul#ant_haul>`\ , except you may divide the duration in 2-hour intervals among the creatures touched.
