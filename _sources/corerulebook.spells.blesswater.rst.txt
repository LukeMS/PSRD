Bless Water
============

\ **School**\  transmutation [good]; \ **Level**\  cleric 1, paladin 1

\ **Casting Time**\  1 minute

\ **Components**\  V, S, M (5 pounds of powdered silver worth 25 gp)

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  flask of water touched

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates (object); :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes (object)

This transmutation imbues a flask (1 pint) of water with positive energy, turning it into :ref:`holy water <corerulebook.equipment#special_substances_and_items>`\ .
