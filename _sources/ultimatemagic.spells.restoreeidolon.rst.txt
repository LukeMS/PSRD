Restore Eidolon
================

\ **School**\  conjuration (healing); \ **Level**\  summoner 3

\ **Casting Time**\  1 minute

\ **Components**\  V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp or 1,000 gp, see text)

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  eidolon touched

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

\ **Saving Throw**\  Will negates (harmless); \ **Spell Resistance**\  yes (harmless)

This spell functions as :ref:`restoration <corerulebook.spells.restoration#restoration>`\ , except it only affects an eidolon.
