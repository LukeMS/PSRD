Beast Shape I
==============

\ **School**\ transmutation (polymorph); \ **Level**\ sorcerer/wizard 3

\ **Casting Time**\ 1 standard action

\ **Components**\ V, S, M (a piece of the creature whose form you plan to assume)

\ **Range**\ personal

\ **Target**\ you

\ **Duration**\ 1 min./level (D)

When you cast this spell, you can assume the form of any Small or Medium creature of the animal type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: climb 30 feet, fly 30 feet (average maneuverability), swim 30 feet, :ref:`darkvision <corerulebook.glossary#darkvision>`\  60 feet, :ref:`low-light vision <corerulebook.glossary#low_light_vision>`\ , and :ref:`scent <corerulebook.glossary#scent>`\ .

\ *Small animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Small animal, you gain a +2 size bonus to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\  and a +1 natural armor bonus.

\ *Medium animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Medium animal, you gain a +2 size bonus to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\  and a +2 natural armor bonus.

.. _`corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_ii`:

Beast Shape II
===============

\ **School**\  transmutation (polymorph); \ **Level**\  sorcerer/wizard 4

This spell functions as :ref:`beast shape I <corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_i>`\ , except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Tiny or Large creature of the animal type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: climb 60 feet, fly 60 feet (good maneuverability), swim 60 feet, :ref:`darkvision <corerulebook.glossary#darkvision>`\  60 feet, :ref:`low-light vision <corerulebook.glossary#low_light_vision>`\ , :ref:`scent <corerulebook.glossary#scent>`\ , grab, pounce, and trip.

\ *Tiny animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Tiny animal, you gain a +4 size bonus to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , a –2 penalty to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , and a +1 natural armor bonus.

\ *Large animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Large animal, you gain a +4 size bonus to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , a –2 penalty to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , and a +4 natural armor bonus.

.. _`corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_iii`:

Beast Shape III
================

\ **School**\ transmutation (polymorph); \ **Level**\ sorcerer/wizard 5

This spell functions as :ref:`beast shape II <corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_ii>`\ , except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Diminutive or Huge creature of the animal type. This spell also allows you to take on the form of a Small or Medium creature of the magical beast type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: burrow 30 feet, climb 90 feet, fly 90 feet (good maneuverability), swim 90 feet, :ref:`blindsense <corerulebook.glossary#blindsight_and_blindsense>`\  30 feet, :ref:`darkvision <corerulebook.glossary#darkvision>`\  60 feet, :ref:`low-light vision <corerulebook.glossary#low_light_vision>`\ , :ref:`scent <corerulebook.glossary#scent>`\ , constrict, ferocity, grab, jet, poison, pounce, rake, trample, trip, and web.

\ *Diminutive animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Diminutive animal, you gain a +6 size bonus to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , a –4 penalty to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , and a +1 natural armor bonus.

\ *Huge animal*\ : If the form you take is that of a Huge animal, you gain a +6 size bonus to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , a –4 penalty to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , and a +6 natural armor bonus.

\ *Small magical beast*\ : If the form you take is that of a Small magical beast, you gain a +4 size bonus to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , and a +2 natural armor bonus.

\ *Medium magical beast*\ : If the form you take is that of a Medium magical beast, you gain a +4 size bonus to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , and a +4 natural armor bonus.

.. _`corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_iv`:

Beast Shape IV
===============

\ **School**\ transmutation (polymorph); \ **Level**\ sorcerer/wizard 6

This spell functions as :ref:`beast shape III <corerulebook.spells.beastshape#beast_shape_iii>`\  except that it also allows you to assume the form of a Tiny or Large creature of the magical beast type. If the form you assume has any of the following abilities, you gain the listed ability: burrow 60 feet, climb 90 feet, fly 120 feet (good maneuverability), swim 120 feet, :ref:`blindsense <corerulebook.glossary#blindsight_and_blindsense>`\  60 feet, :ref:`darkvision <corerulebook.glossary#darkvision>`\  90 feet, :ref:`low-light vision <corerulebook.glossary#low_light_vision>`\ , :ref:`scent <corerulebook.glossary#scent>`\ , tremorsense 60 feet, breath weapon, constrict, ferocity, grab, jet, poison, pounce, rake, rend, roar, spikes, trample, trip, and web. If the creature has immunity or resistance to any elements, you gain resistance 20 to those elements. If the creature has vulnerability to an element, you gain that vulnerability.

\ *Tiny magical beast*\ : If the form you take is that of a Tiny magical beast, you gain a –2 penalty to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , a +8 size bonus to your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , and a +3 natural armor bonus.

\ *Large magical beast*\ : If the form you take is that of a Large magical beast, you gain a +6 size bonus to your :ref:`Strength <corerulebook.gettingstarted#strength>`\ , a –2 penalty on your :ref:`Dexterity <corerulebook.gettingstarted#dexterity>`\ , a +2 size bonus to your :ref:`Constitution <corerulebook.gettingstarted#constitution>`\ , and a +6 natural armor bonus.
