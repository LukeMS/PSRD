Protection from Arrows, Communal
=================================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 3, sorcerer/wizard 3, summoner 3

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`protection from arrows <corerulebook.spells.protectionfromarrows#protection_from_arrows>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-hour intervals among the creatures touched. Once the spell absorbs 10 points of damage per caster level (maximum 100 points) for a subject, the spell's effects end for that subject.
