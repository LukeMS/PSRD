Finger of Death
================

\ **School**\  necromancy [death]; \ **Level**\  druid 8, sorcerer/wizard 7

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Target**\  one creature

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

\ **Saving Throw**\  :ref:`Fortitude <corerulebook.combat#fortitude>`\  partial; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

This spell instantly delivers 10 points of damage per caster level. If the target's :ref:`Fortitude <corerulebook.combat#fortitude>`\  saving throw succeeds, it instead takes 3d6 points of damage + 1 point per caster level. The subject might die from damage even if it succeeds on its saving throw.
