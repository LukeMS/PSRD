Daze Monster
=============

\ **School**\  enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  bard 2, sorcerer/wizard 2

\ **Range**\ medium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)

\ **Target**\ one living creature of 6 HD or less

This spell functions like :ref:`daze <corerulebook.spells.daze#daze>`\ , but it can affect any one living creature of any type. Creatures of 7 or more HD are not affected.
