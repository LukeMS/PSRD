Minor Image
============

\ **School**\  illusion (figment); \ **Level**\  bard 2, sorcerer/wizard 2

\ **Duration**\  concentration + 2 rounds

This spell functions like :ref:`silent image <corerulebook.spells.silentimage#silent_image>`\ , except that \ *minor image*\ includes some minor sounds but not understandable speech.
