Resistance
===========

\ **School**\  abjuration; \ **Level**\  bard 0, cleric 0, druid 0, paladin 1, sorcerer/wizard 0

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, M/DF (a miniature cloak)

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  creature touched

\ **Duration**\  1 minute

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates (harmless); :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes (harmless)

You imbue the subject with magical energy that protects it from harm, granting it a +1 resistance bonus on saves.

\ *Resistance*\ can be made permanent with a :ref:`permanency <corerulebook.spells.permanency#permanency>`\  spell.
