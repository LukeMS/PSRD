Mage Armor
===========

\ **Source**\  :ref:`mage armor <corerulebook.spells.magearmor#mage_armor>`

The armor bonus increases to +6. There is a 50% chance that any critical hit or sneak attack made against the target is negated and treated as a normal hit, as if the target were wearing moderate :ref:`fortification <corerulebook.magicitems.armor#armor_fortification>`\  armor.
