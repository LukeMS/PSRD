Cause Fear
===========

\ **School**\  necromancy [fear, mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  bard 1, cleric 1, sorcerer/wizard 1

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Target**\  one living creature with 5 or fewer HD

\ **Duration**\  1d4 rounds or 1 round; see text

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  partial; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

The affected creature becomes :ref:`frightened <corerulebook.glossary#frightened>`\ . If the subject succeeds on a :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  save, it is :ref:`shaken <corerulebook.glossary#shaken>`\  for 1 round. Creatures with 6 or more HD are immune to this effect. \ *Cause fear*\  counters and dispels :ref:`remove fear <corerulebook.spells.removefear>`\ .
