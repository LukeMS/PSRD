Dominate Monster
=================

\ **School**\  enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  sorcerer/wizard 9

\ **Target**\  one creature

This spell functions like :ref:`dominate person <corerulebook.spells.dominateperson#dominate_person>`\ , except that the spell is not restricted by creature type.
