Animal Aspect
==============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`animal aspect <ultimatecombat.spells.animalaspect#animal_aspect>`

You gain :ref:`low-light vision <bestiary.universalmonsterrules#low_light_vision>`\ , a +2 size bonus to the physical ability score of your choice, and a +1 enhancement bonus to your natural armor. Alternatively, you may cast the non-mythic form of this spell on a touched willing creature.
