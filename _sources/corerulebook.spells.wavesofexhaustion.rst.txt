Waves of Exhaustion
====================

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  sorcerer/wizard 7

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S

\ **Range**\  60 ft.

\ **Area**\  cone-shaped burst

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

\ **Saving Throw**\  no; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

Waves of negative energy cause all living creatures in the spell's area to become :ref:`exhausted <corerulebook.glossary#exhausted>`\ . This spell has no effect on a creature that is already :ref:`exhausted <corerulebook.glossary#exhausted>`\ .
