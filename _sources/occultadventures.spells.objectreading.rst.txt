Object Reading
===============

\ **School**\  divination; \ **Level**\  medium 1, mesmerist 2, occultist 1, psychic 2, spiritualist 2

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  one touched object

\ **Duration**\  concentration, up to 1 round per level (D)

\ **Saving Throw**\  none; \ **Spell Resistance**\  no

You read the psychic impressions left upon an object by emotionally or psychically charged events in the item's history, or the information imprinted by the :ref:`charge object <occultadventures.spells.chargeobject#charge_object>`\  or :ref:`implant false reading <occultadventures.spells.implantfalsereading#implant_false_reading>`\  spells. This spell returns the same information as the psychometry occult skill unlock, but gives 1 piece of information when cast and requires 1 round of concentration per additional piece of information instead of 1 minute. You must still attempt an :ref:`Appraise <corerulebook.skills.appraise#appraise>`\  check to see how much information you gain. You gain a +10 competence bonus on the check.
