Poison
=======

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  cleric 4, druid 3

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, DF

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  living creature touched

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous; see text

\ **Saving Throw**\  :ref:`Fortitude <corerulebook.combat#fortitude>`\  negates; see text; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

Calling upon the venomous powers of natural predators, you infect the subject with a horrible poison by making a successful melee touch attack. This poison deals 1d3 :ref:`Constitution <corerulebook.gettingstarted#constitution>`\  damage per round for 6 rounds. Poisoned creatures can make a :ref:`Fortitude <corerulebook.combat#fortitude>`\  save each round to negate the damage and end the affliction.
