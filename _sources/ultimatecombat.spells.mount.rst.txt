Mount, Communal
================

\ **Level**\ magus 2, sorcerer/wizard 2, summoner 2, witch 2

\ **Effect**\  up to six mounts

 This spell functions like :ref:`mount <corerulebook.spells.mount#mount>`\ , except you can summon up to six light horses or ponies, and you divide the duration in 2-hour increments among the steeds summoned.
