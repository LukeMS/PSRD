Magic Vestment
===============

\ **School**\  transmutation; \ **Level**\  cleric 3

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, DF

\ **Range**\  touch

\ **Target**\  armor or shield touched

\ **Duration**\  1 hour/level

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates (harmless, object); :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes (harmless, object)

You imbue a suit of armor or a shield with an enhancement bonus of +1 per four caster levels (maximum +5 at 20th level).

An outfit of regular clothing counts as armor that grants no :ref:`AC <corerulebook.combat#armor_class>`\  bonus for the purpose of this spell.
