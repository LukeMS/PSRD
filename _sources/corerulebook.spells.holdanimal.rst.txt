Hold Animal
============

\ **School**\  enchantment (compulsion) [mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  druid 2, ranger 2

\ **Components**\ : V, S

\ **Target**\  one animal

This spell functions like :ref:`hold person <corerulebook.spells.holdperson#hold_person>`\ , except that it affects an animal instead of a humanoid.
