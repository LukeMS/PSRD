False Life, Greater
====================

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  alchemist 4, sorcerer/wizard 4, witch 4

This spell functions as :ref:`false life <corerulebook.spells.falselife#false_life>`\ , except you gain temporary hit points equal to 2d10 + 1 point per caster level (maximum +20). The effects of this spell do not stack with those of :ref:`false life <corerulebook.spells.falselife#false_life>`\ .
