Dismissal
==========

\ **School**\  abjuration; \ **Level**\  cleric 4, sorcerer/wizard 5

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, DF

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Target**\  one extraplanar creature

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates; see text; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

This spell forces an extraplanar creature back to its proper plane if it fails a :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  save. If the spell is successful, the creature is instantly whisked away, but there is a 20% chance of actually sending the subject to a plane other than its own.
