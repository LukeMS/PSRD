Dispel Chaos
=============

\ **School**\  abjuration [lawful]; \ **Level**\  cleric 5, paladin 4

This spell functions like :ref:`dispel evil <corerulebook.spells.dispelevil#dispel_evil>`\ , except that you are surrounded by constant, blue lawful energy, and the spell affects chaotic creatures and spells rather than evil ones.
