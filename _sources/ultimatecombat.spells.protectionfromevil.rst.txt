Protection from Evil, Communal
===============================

\ **Level**\  cleric 2, inquisitor 2, paladin 2, sorcerer/wizard 2, summoner 2

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`protection from evil <corerulebook.spells.protectionfromevil#protection_from_evil>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
