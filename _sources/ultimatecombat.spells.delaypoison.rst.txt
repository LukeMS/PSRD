Delay Poison, Communal
=======================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 3, bard 3, cleric 3, druid 3, inquisitor 3, paladin 3, ranger 3, witch 3

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

This spell functions like :ref:`delay poison <corerulebook.spells.delaypoison#delay_poison>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-hour intervals among the creatures touched.
