Darkvision, Greater
====================

\ **School**\  transmutation; \ **Level**\  alchemist 4, antipaladin 4, ranger 4, sorcerer/wizard 4

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

This spell functions as :ref:`darkvision <corerulebook.spells.darkvision#darkvision>`\ , except the target's darkvision has a range of 120 feet.
