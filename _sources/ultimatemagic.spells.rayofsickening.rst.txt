Ray of Sickening
=================

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  cleric 1, druid 1, sorcerer/wizard 1, summoner 1, witch 1

This spell functions as :ref:`ray of exhaustion <corerulebook.spells.rayofexhaustion#ray_of_exhaustion>`\ , except the target is sickened if it fails its save and unaffected if it makes its save.
