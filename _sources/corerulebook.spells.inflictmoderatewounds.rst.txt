Inflict Moderate Wounds
========================

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  cleric 2

This spell functions like :ref:`inflict light wounds <corerulebook.spells.inflictlightwounds#inflict_light_wounds>`\ , except that you deal 2d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +10).

.. _`corerulebook.spells.inflictmoderatewounds#inflict_moderate_wounds_mass`:

Inflict Moderate Wounds, Mass
==============================

\ **School**\  necromancy; \ **Level**\  cleric 6

This spell functions like \ *mass inflict light wounds*\ , except that it deals 2d8 points of damage + 1 point per caster level (maximum +30).
