Order's Wrath
==============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`order's wrath <corerulebook.spells.orderswrath#order_s_wrath>`

The damage dealt to :ref:`chaotic <bestiary.creaturetypes#chaotic_subtype>`\  outsiders increases to 1d10 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d10) and the damage to other chaotic creatures increases to 1d12 points of damage per 2 caster levels (maximum 5d12). The duration that creatures in the area are dazed increases to 2 rounds. Chaotic creatures that succeed at their saves are dazed for 1 round.
