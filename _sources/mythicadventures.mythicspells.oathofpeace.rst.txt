Oath of Peace
==============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`Oath of Peace <advancedplayersguide.spells.oathofpeace#oath_of_peace>`

You don't provoke attacks of opportunity when you attempt :ref:`Heal <corerulebook.skills.heal#heal>`\  checks, cast targeted spells with the harmless descriptor, or use positive energy effects to heal living creatures.
