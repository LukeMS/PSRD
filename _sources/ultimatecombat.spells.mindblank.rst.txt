Mind Blank, Communal
=====================

\ **Level**\  sorcerer/wizard 9, witch 9

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`mind blank <corerulebook.spells.mindblank#mind_blank>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-hour increments among the creatures touched.
