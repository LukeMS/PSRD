Summon Froghemoth
==================

\ **School**\  conjuration (summoning); \ **Level**\  druid 9

\ **Casting Time**\  1 round

\ **Components**\  V, S

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Effect**\  one summoned creature

\ **Duration**\  1 round/level (D)

\ **Saving Throw**\  none; \ **Spell Resistance**\  no

This spell functions as :ref:`summon nature's ally IX <corerulebook.spells.summonnaturesally#summon_nature_s_ally_ix>`\ , except you summon a :ref:`froghemoth <bestiary.froghemoth#froghemoth>`\ .
