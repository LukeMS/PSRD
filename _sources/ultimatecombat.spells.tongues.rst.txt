Tongues, Communal
==================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 4, bard 3, cleric 5, sorcerer/wizard 4, summoner 4, witch 4

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`tongues <corerulebook.spells.tongues#tongues>`\ , except you divide the duration in 10-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
