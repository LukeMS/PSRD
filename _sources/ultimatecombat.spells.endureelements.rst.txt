Endure Elements, Communal
==========================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 3, cleric 2, druid 2, paladin 2, ranger 2, sorcerer/wizard 2

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`endure elements <corerulebook.spells.endureelements#endure_elements>`\ , except you divide the duration in 1-hour increments among the creatures touched.
