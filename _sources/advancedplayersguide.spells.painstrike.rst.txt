Pain Strike
============

\ **School**\ evocation [evil]; \ **Level**\ sorcerer/wizard 3, witch 3

\ **Casting Time**\ 1 standard action

\ **Components**\ V, S

\ **Range**\ close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Target**\ one living creature

\ **Duration**\ 1 round/level (D)

\ **Saving Throw**\ Fortitude negates; \ **Spell Resistance**\ yes

:ref:`Pain strike <advancedplayersguide.spells.painstrike#pain_strike>`\  racks the targeted creature with agony, inflicting 1d6 points of nonlethal damage per round for 1 round per level (maximum 10 rounds). Additionally, the affected creature is sickened for the spell's duration, and the caster gains a +4 circumstance bonus on :ref:`Intimidate <corerulebook.skills.intimidate#intimidate>`\  checks against the target.

Pain Strike, Mass
==================

\ **School**\ evocation [evil]; \ **Level**\ sorcerer/wizard 5, witch 5

.. _`advancedplayersguide.spells.painstrike#targets`:

\ **Targets**\ one living creature/level, no two of which can be more than 30 ft. apart

This spell works like :ref:`pain strike <advancedplayersguide.spells.painstrike#pain_strike>`\ , except as noted above.
