Lesser (Spell Name)
====================

Any spell whose name begins with \ *lesser*\ is alphabetized according to the spell name. Thus, the description of a \ *lesser*\ spell appears near the description of the spell on which it is based. Spell chains that have \ *lesser*\ spells in them include those based on the spells :ref:`confusion <corerulebook.spells.confusion#confusion>`\ , :ref:`geas <corerulebook.spells.geasquest#geas_quest>`\ , :ref:`globe of invulnerability <corerulebook.spells.globeofinvulnerability#globe_of_invulnerability>`\ , :ref:`planar ally <corerulebook.spells.planarally#planar_ally>`\ , :ref:`planar binding <corerulebook.spells.planarbinding#planar_binding>`\ , and :ref:`restoration <corerulebook.spells.restoration#restoration>`\ .
