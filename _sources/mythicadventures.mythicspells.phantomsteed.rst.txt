Phantom Steed
==============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`phantom steed <corerulebook.spells.phantomsteed#phantom_steed>`

The steed's rider automatically succeeds at :ref:`Ride <corerulebook.skills.ride#ride>`\  checks to remain mounted. The mount's hit points increase to 10 plus 2 per caster level, and its speed increases to 30 feet per 2 caster levels (maximum 150 feet).

\ **Augmented (3rd)**\ : If you expend two uses of mythic power, the phantom steed is incorporeal, but its rider can interact with it normally.
