Spider Climb, Communal
=======================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 3, druid 3, sorcerer/wizard 3, summoner 3

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`spider climb <corerulebook.spells.spiderclimb#spider_climb>`\ , except you divide the duration in 10-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
