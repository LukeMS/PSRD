Chaos Hammer
=============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`chaos hammer <corerulebook.spells.chaoshammer#chaos_hammer>`

The damage dealt to :ref:`lawful <bestiary.creaturetypes#lawful_subtype>`\  outsiders increases to 1d10 points of damage per caster level (maximum 10d10), and the damage dealt to other lawful creatures increases to 1d12 points of damage per 2 caster levels (maximum 5d12). The duration of :ref:`slow <corerulebook.spells.slow#slow>`\  increases to 2d6 rounds. Lawful creatures that succeed at their saves are slowed for 1 round.
