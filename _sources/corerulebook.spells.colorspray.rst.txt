Color Spray
============

\ **School**\  illusion (pattern) [mind-affecting]; \ **Level**\  sorcerer/wizard 1

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, S, M (red, yellow, and blue powder or colored sand)

\ **Range**\  15 ft.

\ **Area**\  cone-shaped burst

\ **Duration**\  instantaneous; see text

\ **Saving Throw**\ :ref:`Will <corerulebook.combat#will>`\  negates; :ref:`Spell Resistance <corerulebook.glossary#spell_resistance>`\  yes

A vivid cone of clashing colors springs forth from your hand, causing creatures to become :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\ , perhaps also :ref:`blinded <corerulebook.glossary#blinded>`\ , and possibly knocking them :ref:`unconscious <corerulebook.glossary#unconscious>`\ . Each creature within the cone is affected according to its HD.

\ *2 HD or less*\ : The creature is :ref:`unconscious <corerulebook.glossary#unconscious>`\ , :ref:`blinded <corerulebook.glossary#blinded>`\ , and :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 2d4 rounds, then :ref:`blinded <corerulebook.glossary#blinded>`\  and :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 1d4 rounds, and then :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 1 round. (Only living creatures are knocked :ref:`unconscious <corerulebook.glossary#unconscious>`\ .)

\ *3 or 4 HD*\ : The creature is :ref:`blinded <corerulebook.glossary#blinded>`\  and :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 1d4 rounds, then :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 1 round.

\ *5 or more HD*\ : The creature is :ref:`stunned <corerulebook.glossary#stunned>`\  for 1 round.

Sightless creatures are not affected by \ *color spray*\ .
