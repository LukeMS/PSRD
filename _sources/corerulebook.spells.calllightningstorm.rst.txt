Call Lightning Storm
=====================

\ **School**\  evocation [electricity]; \ **Level**\  druid 5

\ **Range**\  long (400 ft. + 40 ft./level)

This spell functions like :ref:`call lightning <corerulebook.spells.calllightning#call_lightning>`\ , except that each bolt deals 5d6 points of electricity damage (or 5d10 if created outdoors in a stormy area), and you may call a maximum of 15 bolts.
