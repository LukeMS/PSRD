Heroism
========

\ **Source**\  :ref:`heroism <corerulebook.spells.heroism#heroism>`

The morale bonus increases to +4 and applies on all checks, attack rolls, saves, and weapon damage rolls. Allies adjacent to the target gain a +4 morale bonus on saves against :ref:`fear <bestiary.universalmonsterrules#fear>`\  effects.

\ **Augmented (3rd)**\ : If you expend two uses of mythic power, the target can attempt one additional attack per round when taking a full attack action (as if using a :ref:`haste <corerulebook.spells.haste#haste>`\  spell). The target can use this additional attack ability a number of times equal to your tier.
