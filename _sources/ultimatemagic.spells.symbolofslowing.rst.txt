Symbol of Slowing
==================

\ **School**\  transmutation; \ **Level**\  cleric 4, sorcerer/wizard 4, witch 4

\ **Components**\  V, S, M (mercury and phosphorous, plus powdered diamond and opal worth a total of 1,000 gp)

\ **Saving Throw**\  Will negates; \ **Spell Resistance**\  yes

This spell functions as :ref:`symbol of death <corerulebook.spells.symbolofdeath#symbol_of_death>`\ , except all creatures within 60 feet of a \ *symbol of slowing*\  are slowed (as the :ref:`slow <corerulebook.spells.slow#slow>`\  spell) for 1 round per caster level.

\ *Symbol of slowing*\  can be made permanent with a :ref:`permanency <corerulebook.spells.permanency#permanency>`\  spell by a caster of 11th level or higher for the cost of 10,000 gp.
