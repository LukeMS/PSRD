Heightened Awareness
=====================

\ **School**\  divination; \ **Level**\  alchemist 1, bard 1, druid 1, inquisitor 1, ranger 1, shaman 1, sorcerer/wizard 1

\ **Casting Time**\  1 standard action

\ **Components**\  V, M/DF (a coffee bean)

\ **Range**\  personal

\ **Target**\  you

\ **Duration**\  10 minutes/level (D)

You enter a heightened state of awareness that allows you to notice more about your surroundings and recall information effortlessly. You gain a +2 competence bonus on :ref:`Perception <corerulebook.skills.perception#perception>`\  checks and on all :ref:`Knowledge <corerulebook.skills.knowledge#knowledge>`\  checks that you are trained in.

If this spell is active when you have to make an initiative check, you can instantly dismiss this spell and gain a +4 bonus on that check.
