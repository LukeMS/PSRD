Invisibility
=============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`invisibility <corerulebook.spells.invisibility#invisibility>`

The invisible target can't be detected with :ref:`detect magic <corerulebook.spells.detectmagic#detect_magic>`\  or other spells that detect magic auras. The invisibility can't be penetrated, revealed, or dispelled by spells of 2nd level or lower (such as :ref:`see invisibility <corerulebook.spells.seeinvisibility#see_invisibility>`\  or :ref:`glitterdust <corerulebook.spells.glitterdust#glitterdust>`\ ), though :ref:`true seeing <corerulebook.spells.trueseeing#true_seeing>`\  and :ref:`dust of appearance <corerulebook.magicitems.wondrousitems#dust_of_appearance>`\  can reveal the invisible target's presence.

\ **Augmented (3rd)**\ : If you expend two uses of mythic power, the invisible target is undetectable by blindsense, blindsight, scent, and tremorsense.
