Resist Energy, Communal
========================

\ **Level**\  alchemist 3, cleric 3, druid 3, inquisitor 3, paladin 3, ranger 3, sorcerer/wizard 3, summoner 3

\ **Target**\  creatures touched

 This spell functions like :ref:`resist energy <corerulebook.spells.resistenergy#resist_energy>`\ , except you divide the duration in 10-minute intervals among the creatures touched.
