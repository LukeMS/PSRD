Flame Strike
=============

\ **Source**\  :ref:`flame strike <corerulebook.spells.flamestrike#flame_strike>`

The damage dealt increases to 1d8 per caster level (maximum 15d8). The area increases to a 20-foot-radius, 40-foot-high cylinder. You may have the divine half of the damage affect only one specific creature type (as the :ref:`bane <corerulebook.magicitems.weapons#weapons_bane>`\  weapon quality).
