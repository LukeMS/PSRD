Summon Minor Ally
==================

\ **School**\  conjuration (summoning); \ **Level**\  druid 1, ranger 1

\ **Casting Time**\  1 round

\ **Components**\  V, S, DF

\ **Range**\  close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

\ **Effect**\  1d3 summoned creatures

\ **Duration**\  1 round/level (D)

\ **Saving Throw**\  none; \ **Spell Resistance**\  no

This spell functions as :ref:`summon nature's ally I <corerulebook.spells.summonnaturesally#summon_nature_s_ally_i>`\ , except you can summon 1d3 Tiny or smaller animals, such as bats, lizards, monkeys, rats, ravens, toads, or weasels. The summoned animals must all be the same type of creature.
