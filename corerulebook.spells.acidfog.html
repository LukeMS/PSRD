Acid Fog¶

School conjuration (creation) [acid]; Level sorcerer/wizard 6

Casting Time 1 standard action

Components V, S, M (powdered peas and an animal hoof)

Rangemedium (100 ft. + 10 ft./level)

Effect fog spreads in 20-ft. radius, 20 ft. high

Duration 1 round/level

Saving Throw none; Spell Resistance no

Acid fogcreates a billowing mass of misty vapors like the solid fog spell. In addition to slowing down creatures and obscuring sight, this spell’s vapors are highly acidic. Each round on your turn, starting when you cast the spell, the fog deals 2d6 points of acid damage to each creature and object within it.
