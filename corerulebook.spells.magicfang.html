Magic Fang¶

School transmutation; Level druid 1, ranger 1

Casting Time 1 standard action

Components V, S, DF

Range touch

Target living creature touched

Duration 1 min./level

Saving ThrowWill negates (harmless); Spell Resistance yes (harmless)

Magic fanggives one natural weapon or unarmed strike of the subject a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls. The spell can affect a slam attack, fist, bite, or other natural weapon. The spell does not change an unarmed strike’s damage from nonlethal damage to lethal damage.

Magic fangcan be made permanent with a permanency spell.

Magic Fang, Greater¶

School transmutation; Level druid 3, ranger 3

Range close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)

Target one living creature

Duration 1 hour/level

This spell functions like magic fang, except that the enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls is +1 per four caster levels (maximum +5). This bonus does not allow a natural weapon or unarmed strike to bypass damage reduction aside from magic.

Alternatively, you may imbue all of the creature’s natural weapons with a +1 enhancement bonus (regardless of your caster level).

Greater magic fangcan be made permanent with a permanency spell.
